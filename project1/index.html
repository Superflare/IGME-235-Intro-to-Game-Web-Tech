Game Developer and Designer

I am a programmer that enjoys the challenge of solving complex problems and adding creativity to go above and beyond expectations. I have experience in C#, Unity, and Visual Studio but am very interested in learning various other languages, tools, and programs. I like to focus my programming skills into game development because I believe in how impactful video games can be to people's lives and I love how games strive to bring fun to their players. I am excited by the prospect of working with a team full of talented people and helping to create something that is used by many people, learning as much as I can along the way.
